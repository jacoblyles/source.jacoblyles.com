Coursera course age distribution

I'm taking a quantum computation class at Coursera. On the forum for the course there was an informal age poll posted. I was surprised by an unexpectedly large population of people responding aged over 40 and under 18. This made me curious - is Coursera primarily being used to satisfy curiosity only by people far away from college years, or by people looking to supplement or replace a college education? I used some web tools to find out.
